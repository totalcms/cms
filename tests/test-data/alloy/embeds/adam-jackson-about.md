Both my parents were professional photographers, so naturally I spent more than 30 years in IT, delivering innovative technology solutions for businesses of all shapes & sizes, after they both talked me out of following in their footsteps.

Like Julia, I’ve had a life-long passion for creative photography. I also love gadgets and aviation, and I built this website. I’m a CAA-qualified drone pilot, so we are able to offer a full range of aerial photography and videography, in addition to ground-based services and full post-production capabilities.

As Confucius said, *“Choose a job you love, and you will never have to work a day in your life”*.
