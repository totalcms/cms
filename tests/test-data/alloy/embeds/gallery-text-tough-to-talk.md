## Tough to Talk
<br>
We're proud to support the [Tough to Talk](https://toughtotalk.com) Men's Suicide and Mental Health charity.<br>

We were delighted to be invited to work with the inspirational Founder and "Top Banana", [Steve Whittle](https://www.linkedin.com/in/steve-whittle-09a9029/) to produce powerful imagery for a series of presentations that really get this vitally important message across.

There's a mix of portraits that show both the light and dark side of mental health. We also proposed some creative shots depicting that whilst all may appear well on the outside, it can be very different on the inside. Finally, there are some images that illustrate Steve's own personal story.

**Please make sure that your screen brightness is set so that white appears genuinely WHITE (including TrueTone, auto-adjustment, etc.) - otherwise, the photos may not appear accurate.**
