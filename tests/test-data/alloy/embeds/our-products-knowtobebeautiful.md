We look for previously-loved items, to offer unique gifts, chosen purely for their beauty. Anything beautiful makes us happy, so we end up with a wide range of different things. Some may be made of precious metals, have gem stones, age or rarity, but others are just beautiful and deserve some more love.

We value (and price) items based on their beauty and very little else. 

Every item is individually numbered (after all, every item is unique) and comes with as much information as we can find. We aim to offer a fascinating range of vintage gifts.

Some things have been loved a little too well over the years and need more attention before they can be sent back out into the world. Our ‘Colour’ range has been given that extra attention, usually with the addition of powder coating to really zing things up and make every item truly unique.

Of course beauty is subjective, so you may not always agree with us! But we hope you find plenty of things in our [gift shop](https://knowtobebeautiful.co.uk/etsy) to love, and you choose to re-home something you yourself know to be beautiful.
