Our items are special treasures and make wonderful gifts. Of course, when you buy something as a gift, you need it to be beautifully presented. We believe that one of the main reasons people buy expensive new things is because they like the packaging!

But we're also trying to stop waste, so we don't want to go overboard. Who really needs more plush presentation boxes at the back of the wardrobe - too good to throw away but really not needed? 

So we keep things elegant and classy, but not over the top. And of course everything is packaged safely for posting.

Delivery is normally within five working days, but if you need something more urgently please ask, and we'll let you know if it can be quicker.
