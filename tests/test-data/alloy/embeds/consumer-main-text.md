## Filming Beautiful Homes
<br>
We love to film beautiful homes, especially those with interesting histories.<br><br>
If you’re selling an historic home, we’ll produce a high-quality video that showcases the property, combining today’s beauty with the past that has contributed so much to the character. We want to bring your property to life, and captivate prospective buyers.<br><br>
Our standard service includes HD video, drone footage (where possible), graphics and fully-licenced music, all edited to produce an engaging insight into the property.<br><br>
We usually make three videos for each historic house:<br><br>

* ###### Showcase Video
A 2-to-3-minute film for use in property listings.
<br>

* ###### Highlights Video
A 30 second teaser, perfect for social media.
<br>

* ###### Historic Exploration Video
A mini-documentary, with voiceover, that shows more detail and explores the history of the property, using census and electoral roll information.
<br><br>

Why not have a browse through some of the films we’ve already made for some wonderful historic homes.
