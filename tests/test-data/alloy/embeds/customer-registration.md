## Customer Registration
<br>
Please register with our secure portal, so that we can share your files with you when they're ready.
<br><br>
When registered, you will receive an email with your Username and Password.
<br><br>
**Please keep these details safe, as they are stored in a secure, encrypted format, and we have no way to retrieve them.**
<br><br>
We take your privacy and security very seriously, and will never ask you for any personal details other than your name and email address. Your details will never be shared with 3rd parties.
<br><br>
If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to [contact](https://ktbb.co.uk/contact/) us.
