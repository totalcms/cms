## Frequently Asked Questions
<br>

Here, we'll attempt to answer the most frequently asked questions we get. Clearly, some things fall squarely into the "How long is a piece of string?" category, so a definitive answer is impossible, but we'll do our best. Of course, we'll add to this over time.<br><br>

#### What is product photography?
Product photography is the art of capturing high-quality images that showcase products in their best light. It involves professional techniques and equipment to highlight the unique features, details, and aesthetics of various items.<br><br>

#### Why is professional product photography important?
Professional product photography plays a crucial role in presenting products effectively. It enhances brand credibility, creates a strong visual impact, and helps drive sales. High-quality images build trust, showcase product details, and evoke emotions, enticing potential customers to make a purchase.<br><br>

#### How can professional product photography benefit my business?
Investing in professional product photography can bring numerous benefits to your business. It elevates your brand image, increases customer engagement, and helps you stand out from competitors. Exceptional product images can improve online visibility, drive conversions, and boost your overall marketing efforts.<br><br>

#### Do you offer different types of product photography?
Yes, we provide a range of product photography services tailored to your specific needs. Our offerings include e-commerce photography, lifestyle photography, 360° product photography, and more. Each type is designed to highlight different aspects of your products and cater to various marketing platforms.<br><br>

#### What is the process for working with you?
Our process begins with a detailed consultation to understand your brand, product requirements, and desired outcomes. We then plan the photo shoot, including styling, props, and any specific requests. During the shoot, we'll meticulously capture your products. Finally, we provide you with the edited, high-resolution images for your approval and use.<br><br>

#### How quickly will you complete my project?
The turnaround time can vary depending on the project scope and complexity. We strive to deliver the final images within a reasonable timeframe, typically ranging from a few days to a couple of weeks. Rest assured, we prioritise quality and ensure that you receive exceptional images within an agreed-upon timeframe. #### How will I receive the finished images?
We'll supply your photos electronically, per the agreed specification - web resolution JPG, PNG or WebP files will be sized to your requirements, and we can provide you with lossless high-resolution TIFF files to archive for future use.<br><br>

#### Can you accommodate large-scale product photography projects?
Absolutely! We have the capacity and expertise to handle projects of varying scales. Whether you have a small batch of products or a large inventory, we can efficiently capture high-quality images that meet your specifications and deadlines.<br><br>

#### Can you provide custom editing or retouching services?
Yes, we offer custom editing and retouching services to enhance your product images further. We can make adjustments, remove imperfections, optimise colors, and create a consistent visual style, ensuring your products look their absolute best.<br><br>

#### Can I have the RAW files, so I can edit them?
Sorry, no. Photography is an art, and 3<sup>rd</sup> party editing is specifically prohibited by our contract, in order to ensure that our work is not misrepresented. Having taken the time to work with you to agree a detailed scope of work, you can rest assured that the final images we supply will align with your vision.<br><br>

#### Will I own the copyright for the images?
Our contract grants an *unlimited usage licence* for your images (although you may not edit or resell them, for example to a stock photography website). We retain the copyright, but will only use your images as part of our portfolio and/or for marketing purposes.

*NB. Full copyright ownership can be made available by separate negotiation.*<br><br>
