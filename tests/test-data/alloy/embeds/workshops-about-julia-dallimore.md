### Julia Dallimore
<br>
![](https://ktbb.co.uk/image-uploads/Julia_Portrait_Jan_2023-400x400_0.jpg)
<br><br>
For over 30 years I worked in marketing, specialising in direct response.  I’ve worked with start-ups and FTSE 250 companies, managing multi-million pound budgets to drive cost effective response.  I’ve managed award-winning creative and media agencies, commissioned many TV adverts and a full range of other digital and offline media activities, events and publications.

But I’ve always had a passion for photography and video, so I now apply my big-budget experience to deliver results for organisations with more limited resources.  

Getting hands-on with jobs I’d always ‘outsourced’ is an absolute joy.  I only wish I’d done it sooner!
