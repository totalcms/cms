## Photographic Services
<br>
We offer a full range of photography, either to complement our videography, or as a standalone service.<br><br>
As with our videos, we put a huge amount of effort into the entire production process, from clarifying your needs, taking the time to ensure that the material we capture is of the highest possible quality, to our fastidious approach to post-production, where we hand-craft every single image.<br><br>
As you'd expect, our pricing is based on your exact requirements, so please get in [touch](https://ktbb.co.uk/contact/) so we can discuss your vision, and how we can deliver results for you and your business.
<br><br>

### Product Photography
<br>
We specialise in creative product photography, suitable for advertising campaigns, websites and social media.  We can follow your ideas, or just let us have your product and a general idea of what you're looking for, and leave the rest to us!

We can also do simple, clear product photography, for use in ecommerce.  The product is carefully lit and shown from all angles, against a classic white background.  Your buyers will get an accurate, comprehensive impression of your products, to maximise your conversion rates.
<br><br>
You can view a gallery of our product photos [here](https://ktbb.co.uk/gallery/product/).
<br><br>
And, of course, we can also produce videos. Whether of individual products, or to showcase your range, we'll make your offering really pop!

### Portraiture
<br>
We offer stunning portraiture, creating striking images to put a smile on your face.  All our photography is done in your home.  We'll bring the necessary lighting, backdrops, etc, so you can feel perfectly relaxed and change your costume as often as you like!

As with our product photography, we can also provide simpler, more formal business portraits suitable for your website, social media, etc.  We'll shoot in your workplace, so disruption to your working day is kept to a minimum.
<br><br>
You can view a gallery of our portrait photos [here](https://ktbb.co.uk/gallery/portrait/).
