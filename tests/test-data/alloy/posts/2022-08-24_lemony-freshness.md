It's been a while!

What have we been up to?

Well, following an extended break due to moving from Surrey to Derbyshire, we're now fully back up and running. This week, we've finally managed to get into our new Studio!

Having completed some lovely Red Wine shots - which I'll probably post about another time - we decided to do something bright & fresh. And challenging.

We had great fun dropping the Shower Gel and Lemons into the water tank. It got very wet and splashy in the Studio... Several hundred shots later (yes, really - Products dropped in water tend not to cooperate in showing their best side!), we had enough in the can to move onto the 'dry shot' of the product. Why did we need a dry shot? Well, it's pretty much impossible to get the right lighting through water, due to refraction. The challenge was that it needed to be positioned at exactly the same height, angle(s), and distance to camera, in order to be able to be composited.
