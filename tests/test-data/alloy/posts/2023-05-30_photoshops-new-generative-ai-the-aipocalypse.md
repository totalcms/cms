Along with every other photographer on the planet, we've been having some fun exploring AI!

The first photo is a shot of a delicious Toffee Vodka by Kin Vodka, which we found at a food fair a couple of weeks ago. This was done in our studio, with props and careful lighting to make sure the bottle and the brand really shine.

![](https://ktbb.co.uk/image-uploads/Toffee_Vodka_original_800.jpg)

The second photo is the result of Photoshop's new Generative Fill AI, asking it to place the same shot into an old bar.

We think it looks pretty good!

![](https://ktbb.co.uk/image-uploads/Toffee_Vodka_800.jpg)

The feature is in beta at the moment and can't be used for commercial shots.

Who knows how it will pan out in terms of licensing, as the AI is basically finding a stock shot to base its work on.

But, in principle, we like where this is heading. It doesn't take away the need for a high quality photograph to start with, but it opens up creative opportunities that may otherwise require huge budgets for location shoots.

And it's great fun to play with!
