Happy Valentine's Day! Can't believe it's been two months since we last posted a blog, but it's been incredibly busy. Hopefully, when reading the title you're hearing the Danish Beer advert voiceover, rather than Clarkson...

Anyway, this bottle of perfume was WAY harder to photograph than it might appear. Black on black - what could possibly go wrong!

Quite a lot, really...

First, the sparkly detail on the bottle needed focus-stacking, whilst ensuring a beautiful graduated light from the right-hand side, as well as a more subtle edge-light on the left - all whilst ensuring a perfectly black background.

Then, how did we get the lovely light shining through the window in the bottle? Another completely separate shot, with some custom lighting, carefully aimed to shine through, without messing up the clear reflection of the bottle on the perspex tabletop!

Oh, and all the text on the window and around the lid needed to be clearly lit, perfectly in focus, and legible.

Finally, all the separate shots were composited together into a single photo, to give the (we think) very high-class image, below.

![](https://ktbb.co.uk/image-uploads/Black_Opium-800.jpg)

The final result might look simple, but it took hours of work.
