As the festive season approaches, we're reminded that the art of product photography is not just about capturing an image, but about telling a story that evokes emotion and resonates with the viewer. Today, we want to share the creative journey behind two of our recent shots that encapsulate the essence of the holidays with Withers Gin.

Their Gingerbread and Winter Spice Gin is absolutely delicious, and *perfect* for this time of year!

**Conjuring Warmth with a Golden Hue**

![](https://ktbb.co.uk/image-uploads/Ginger_Gin_Baubles-800.jpg)

The first shot was all about that warm, golden glow – the kind that fills you with a sense of comfort during the winter holidays. We aimed to create a scene that not only highlighted the beautiful design of the Withers Gin bottle but also enveloped it in the warmth of the season.

Creating a black backdrop to enhance the golden tones, we back-lit the bottle to create a luminous effect. Strategically placed lighting was diffused and set to just the right intensity to mimic the soft, warm light of the golden hour.

Decorative Christmas baubles were chosen for their matte and reflective surfaces, which interact with the light differently, adding depth to the composition. Golden snowflakes, added in post, brought a sense of festive movement, and frame the bottle, drawing the viewer's eye to the star of the show.

A low aperture captured the bottle with crystal-clear sharpness, while also creating a soft bokeh of the background elements. This ensured the product remained the hero of the story.

**Weaving a Magical Winter's Tale**

![](https://ktbb.co.uk/image-uploads/Ginger_GIn_House-800.jpg)

For the second image, we wanted to go beyond the product and craft a scene that tells a tale of winter wonder. The goal was to transport the viewer to a cozy, enchanted place where Withers Gin is the preferred spirit of choice.

We chose a reflective base, and added a ripple-effect in post, to give a waterside feel. A combination of diffused studio lighting and fairy lights was employed to create a soft, inviting ambiance, while a hazer machine gently layered the set with a misty, dreamlike quality.

A miniature gingerbread house and a twisted wooden figure, with twinkling lights, were positioned to frame the gin bottle. These elements worked together to suggest a narrative – perhaps an enchanting fairytale, or a festive gathering of good friends.

We balanced the scene with a sprinkle of snow, falling gently across the landscape, adding to the whimsical feel of the moment.

Both shots were the result of meticulous planning, creative experimentation, and a deep understanding of the way light interacts with different textures and materials, as well as our usual attention-to-detail with compositing and post-processing. The end result? Two distinct, yet equally enchanting representations of Withers Gin, ready to stand out in a crowded market.

The joy of product photography lies in the ability to transform the ordinary into the extraordinary, and we're always excited to push the boundaries of what can be achieved with a camera and a vision.
