We're both very excited for Christmas 2021. After last year's disappointing last-minute lockdown, it's going to be brilliant to be able to get together with our families this year - we hope!

Having a couple of days free between projects, we decided to do a Christmas-themed shoot for our cards this year.

It was great fun, getting the studio prepared, dressing the set - tree, lights, reindeer, sleigh, baubles and presents - safe to say that we're both fully in the Christmas spirit... (nothing to do with the fizz consumed, honest!)

Really happy with the final edit, which should be back from our printers in a day or two, so family and friends can look forward to receiving them soon!
