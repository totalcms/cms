It was an absolute honour to be asked by the lovely Sarah Withers to capture the press launch of Withers Gin's delicious, limited edition "I'll Remember You" Gin, to commemorate the 80th anniversary of the D-Day Normandy Landings, yesterday.

While we were there, we got a beautiful shot of the new Gin, outside by the river.

![](https://ktbb.co.uk/image-uploads/Withers_at_Darleys-3-800.jpg)

The Gin was crafted in collaboration with the D-Day Darlings, who also feature on the label, and we were treated to the amazing voice of Katie Ashby, their lead singer. Julia only cried twice!!

The setting was perfect, the weather was beautiful, and the Gin was divine!

Under normal circumstances, you'll find us in a darkened studio, where we can completely shape and control the lighting, rather than harnessing the Sun (not to mention that we don't usually 'shoot' people!). But this shoot was a joy and we're really proud of the results.

We love to create hero shots that make any product look beautiful.
