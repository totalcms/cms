Last week, we were given the amazing opportunity to create a video for a stunning period townhouse in London, which was coming onto the market.  The estate agent asked us for a 3-minute showcase video, including drone footage.  A truly amazing opportunity!

We prepared really carefully for our first ‘proper’ real estate shoot and were excited and nervous, in equal measure.  We chose the shoot date based on the weather forecast, and for once, it didn’t let us down.  A stunning day, with clouds so beautiful they looked slightly unreal!

We were a bit worried we wouldn’t be able to fly the drone.  The home is in the very heart of London and just 100m from the Central London ‘no-fly zone’.  Flying there is totally legal (with the qualifications that Adam has), but our drones have GPS geo-locking that stops you flying where you shouldn’t.  A fabulous idea, but not always accurate, so we may have found ourselves unable to take off.  With travel restrictions in place, we couldn’t even try it out before the day of the shoot, so it was rather unnerving!  But, just like the weather, everything was on our side, the drone flew perfectly, and we got some incredible footage.

We’re really happy with our first shoot and edit.  There are a couple of things we’d do a little differently, but we learnt so much and the Estate Agent is really happy.

In the end their Agent Network designers cut the video down a little and replaced most of the captions to match their house style - we weren’t given any brand guidelines to work to, or we would have done so.  But it’s still our video, live on Zoopla, Rightmove, etc. and we’re really proud!

We can’t wait to do the next one, and are still offering some free videos of interesting properties, so do [get in touch](https://ktbb.co.uk/contact/) to have a chat about your requirements.
