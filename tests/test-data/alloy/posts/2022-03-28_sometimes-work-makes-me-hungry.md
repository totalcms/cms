Oh, we had SO much fun with this shoot!

Inspired by some of the Old Masters, we decided to create a Still Life that was decadent and 'painterly'. Something that was more about the Art than the Products, and that would make beautiful prints, which we think would grace any wall.

Selecting the foods to co-star in the shoot was a joy - fabulous cheeses, charcuterie, olives, bread, crackers, fruits and (of course) red wine. It was also a good opportunity to gather together some of the eclectic vintage products from our sister company, [Know to be Beautiful](https://knowtobebeautiful.co.uk/etsy), to star in the photos, producing such a stunning result.

![](https://ktbb.co.uk/image-uploads/Heaven_1-800.jpg)

For an encore, I lobbied to do a cake shot - well, after such a decadent spread, it would be rude not to eat cake, right?!

We're really happy with the way this one turned out as well.

![](https://ktbb.co.uk/image-uploads/Heaven_2-800.jpg)

With both photos, getting the lighting just right was so important. Getting everything lit properly is a challenge when the overall ambiance of the image is designed to be dark. We ended up using our main studio light, along with a secondary strobe to subtly light the background, a speedlite to fill in some shadows, and a projection light with an adjustable iris in the second shot, to light some of the cakes.

**And I'm happy to report that it all tasted every bit as good as it looked...**
